ABOUT US

Years of Service, Moments of Joy!

Welcome to Kingsukh Guest House, your peaceful escape nestled amidst the natural charm of Purulia. Surrounded by the serene beauty of Biharinath Hill, Baranti Hill, Susunia Hill, Joychandi Hill, Garhpanchkot, Baranti Dam, Maithon Dam, and the picturesque Panchat Dam, our guest house offers the perfect blend of comfort, tranquility, and nature. Whether you're exploring the hidden gems of Purulia or simply looking to unwind, Kingsukh Guest House promises a stay full of warmth, simplicity, and heartfelt hospitality. Come stay with us and create memories that stay with you — long after the journey ends.
